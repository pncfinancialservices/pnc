Say Goodbye to Surprise Overdraft Fees

Introducing Low Cash Mode® in the PNC Mobile App[2]

Many banks will charge you multiple overdraft fees a day. Others may promise no overdraft fees but will return all items leaving you with unpaid bills. 

Built on patents-pending technology, Virtual Wallet’s digital tools now include Low Cash Mode℠, a new, game-changing tech that helps you avoid overdraft fees and take control when your balance gets low or negative.

  • Extra Time will show you how much time you have left - which will be at least 24 hours - to bring your available Spend account balance to at least zero before you are charged overdraft fees.[3,4] 
  • Payment Control gives you the option to choose to pay or return certain individual checks, and payments made using your routing and checking account numbers (ACH transactions)[5], when your balance is negative. 
  • Intelligent Alerts notify you when your Spend account balance drops below your threshold so that you can take action.[6]
